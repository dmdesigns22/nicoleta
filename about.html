ABOUT

Nicoletta Zarifi (b. 1997, Athens, GR) is a visual storyteller based in Athens. She is studying Film at the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. Her work currently focuses on analogue photography, with a cinematic aesthetic, fashion-based and influenced by the people and the different places she visits, but mainly by her base, Athens. Her photography is characterized by natural light, embossed textures, dynamic faces and personalities she is searching for, “telling” their stories, while blended in their equally remarkable surroundings.
